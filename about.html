About Bad Cat Hot Sauce

Small-batch fermented hot sauce from Taos, New Mexico — built on bold flavor, high-quality ingredients, and a love for heat that actually means something.

Bad Cat Hot Sauce was born from a fiery passion for bold flavors, adorable cats, and the art of fermentation. Based in the heart of Taos, New Mexico, we craft small-batch hot sauces inspired by the landscapes, culture, and slightly weird magic of our mountain town.

Our Philosophy

We don’t make sauces just to be hot — we make them to taste alive. Every batch is carefully fermented to build depth, complexity, and character before the heat ever hits your tongue.

What We Believe

We support local farmers, use high-quality ingredients, and embrace sustainable practices wherever possible. Food should have personality — and honestly, so should hot sauce.

From Taos to Your Table

From our kitchen in New Mexico to tables across the country, Bad Cat Hot Sauce is made to bring flavor, excitement, and a little controlled chaos to your meals.
