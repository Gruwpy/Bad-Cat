About Bad Cat Hot Sauce

Bad Cat Hot Sauce, based in Taos, New Mexico, creates small-batch, fermented hot sauces made with high-quality ingredients and bold flavor.

Born from a love of fermentation, cats, and heat, we craft sauces inspired by the landscapes of New Mexico.

Every bottle is made in small batches with real ingredients and real character.
