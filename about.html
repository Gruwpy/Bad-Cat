About Bad Cat Hot Sauce

Bad Cat Hot Sauce was born from a fiery passion for bold flavors, adorable cats, and a love for fermentation. Based in Taos, New Mexico, we craft small-batch sauces with big personality.

Every bottle is made with high-quality ingredients and a slow fermentation process that builds deep, layered flavor.

We believe food should never be boring. Stay spicy.
