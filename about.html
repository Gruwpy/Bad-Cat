About Bad Cat Hot Sauce

Bad Cat Hot Sauce was born from a passion for bold flavors, small-batch fermentation, and the weird energy of Taos, New Mexico.

Every bottle is crafted with care, heat, and a focus on deep flavor—not just spice. We believe hot sauce should enhance food, not overpower it.

From local farmers markets to your table, Bad Cat is built to bring personality, heat, and a little chaos to every bite.
